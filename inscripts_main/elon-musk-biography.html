Ashlee Vance's

Elon Musk (Biography)

Summary by Bastav Kakoty
  • March 31, 2022

Introduction -

This book by Ashlee Vance is the official biography of the creator of Tesla, SolarCity, SpaceX and the ultimate visionary and one of the most successful Elon Musk. The book is based on over 30 hours of conversation time between author Ashlee Vance and Elon himself where he highlights his childhood, how he makes decisions, how he established several companies and his goals of saving humanity.

Summary -

The summary is about the lessons that we can learn from Elon’s life in one sentence. If you want to learn more and read about his life then you should definitely read the book.

Key Lessons from Elon’s Life -

1. We should spend most of our time figuring out what questions to ask, rather than just answering them. Right questions can bring tons of opportunities.
2. We should accomplish our goals with a success-through-determination approach, which means sticking with things long enough until they really work out.
3. If we can unite all of our works and projects under the roof of one massive goal, we will leave an enduring impact on the world and its people.
4. Success doesn’t automatically improve our life. We sometimes tend to think that If we get ridiculously successful like Elon Musk or Steve Jobs, we will leave a happier life but Elon himself doesn’t suggest his lifestyle to others as it's pretty hectic and pressurized.
5. Everyone will be amazed that Elon Musk is a space scientist and he doesn’t have any degree in that, he just read all the necessary books for a space scientist and gathered the knowledge on his own. This shows the power of reading books.
6. We must have a clear vision or a clear goal of all the stuff we are doing in our lives otherwise we will get stuck in an endless loop.

Conclusion & Opinion -

Elon's life is filled with ups and downs as our everyone’s life. But the one thing that separates us from him is his vision and his ability to work on it at any cost. He is (as of March 2022) the richest man in the world and maybe the most successful ever. It is not possible to summarize his life in this short summary, his life story is very aspiring and filled with learning for each one of us. That’s why I will ask each and everyone to buy this book from the link below and read it .
